About Me

I am a researcher in machine learning and natural language processing. My focus is in computational social science and specifically in understanding how recent advancements in NLP can be applied to answer questions in social sciences.

I worked on problems including language modeling and understanting, topic modeling and supervised/ unsupervised learning in the industry. I also did some work in consulting and program management in the public sector. I completed my masters in Information Mangement at University of Maryland, College Park and I have a bachelor in engineering in Computer Science from Damascus University.
